About PickPix

PickPix is a Wallpaper app that provides its users with HD Wallpapers for their iOS Devices. PickPix is open for suggestions and for any other concers you can email us at pickpixapp@gmail.com .

How to use the app

The app has categories for the convinience of its users to navigate to their favourite wallpaper category. In addition, if you like a wallpaper and do not wish to save it instantly to your photo albums you can press the "star" on the top right of the wallpaper at that wallpaper will be saved into your favourite wallpapers in the app. To navigate to your favourite wallpapers click on the menu icon (bottom left) and go to the Favourites from the tab bar.
